Ladies and gents, I'm thrilled to introduce a nifty new feature designed to give you greater control over your trading strategies: the Volume Filter. Let's dive right in!

## Understanding the Basics

The core purpose of the Volume Filter is to keep you from trading low-volume tokens inadvertently. Now, don't get me wrong. Lower-volume tokens can sometimes offer enticing opportunities, but they also carry a higher degree of risk, mainly due to low liquidity. It could be akin to walking on a tightrope with no safety net! This feature acts as that safety net, allowing you to focus on tokens with a more substantial trading volume and greater liquidity.

## How Does It Work?

The Volume Filter operates based on token trading volume data across all exchanges. This data is updated hourly, ensuring you always deal with the most current information.

You can use the Volume Filter when setting up your multi-coin trading bots on Gainium. When activated, it limits your bot to trading only the top tokens by trading volume. And how does it define 'top'? Well, that's up to you.

You could choose to have your bot trade only the top 25, or maybe you feel more adventurous and go for the top 100. You could even punch in a custom number, like 500, and have your bot trade only the top 500 tokens by trading volume. The choice is entirely in your hands.

## Why is this Feature Important?

In this rapid, ever-changing world of crypto trading, sifting through and focusing on tokens with more substantial trading volumes can be a game-changer. This feature allows you to navigate through the sea of tokens and zero in on those that meet your specific volume criteria. This strategic maneuvering can drastically reduce your exposure to less liquid coins, which can sometimes be harder to buy or sell without impacting the token's price.
